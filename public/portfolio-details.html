Anti-Illegal Drugs Campaign: Musika Kontra Droga

Last December 14, 2019, the Sangguniang Kabataan Council conducted an Anti- Illegal Drugs Campaign with the theme: Musika Kontra Droga. Bands from all over Sta. Lucia got to show their craft on music, at the same time, joined the fight against illegal drugs. After the seminar, the participants were given t-shirts as freebies.
